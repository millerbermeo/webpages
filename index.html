Do You Like MMA?

MMA or Mixed Martial Arts is the fusion of different styles of traditional martial arts. The best aspects of disciplines such as boxing, Brazilian jiu jitsu, wrestling, kickboxing, muay thai, karate and judo, were mixed to form an effective and complete fighting style, where two athletes face each other in a cage under strict rules that the They protect from severe damage, but at the same time allow so much action that they have made MMA the fastest growing sport in the world.

Khabib 'The Eagle' Nurmagomedoz

Khabib Nurmagomedov (born 20 September 1988) is a Russian professional mixed martial artist. He is currently signed to the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) where he is the current reigning UFC Lightweight Champion. With 28 wins and no losses, he holds the longest active undefeated streak in MMA. As of 9 September 2020, he is #2 in the UFC men's pound-for-pound rankings.
Nurmagomedov is the first Muslim to win a UFC title. He is a two-time Combat Sambo World Champion. Trained in sambo, judo, and wrestling, Nurmagomedov's style of grappling has made him one of the most dominant athletes in the history of MMA. As of September 2019, he is the most followed Russian celebrity on Instagram.
